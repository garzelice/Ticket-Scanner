## Design Context

### Users
Event staff operating in fast-paced, high volume, often low-light environments. They need to scan tickets quickly and reliably without squinting at the screen.

### Brand Personality
Industrial, utilitarian, fast, and high contrast. The app should feel like a precision tool focused purely on speed and efficiency rather than decorative elements.

### Aesthetic Direction
System Adaptive (supporting both light and dark modes equally). The design relies on high contrast colors for instant legibility, clear status indicators (success/error/offline), and a brutally functional aesthetic.

### Design Principles
1. **Optimize for Speed**: Prioritize massive touch targets for the primary action (scanning) and instant, unmistakable visual feedback.
2. **High Contrast Legibility**: Make critical information (ticket status, sync state) immediately obvious at a glance through bold typography and clear iconography.
3. **Intentional Rhythm**: Use generous spacing around primary actions to prevent mis-taps, but maintain a dense, scannable rhythm for the list of tickets.
4. **Resilient Feedback**: Error states and offline indicators must be prominent but not block the core workflow.
